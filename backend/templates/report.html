Recommended Remediations

Data

Rebalance with SMOTE

Synthetically oversample minority subgroups to equalize representation before training. Effective when DP Δ > 0.10.

Data

Remove proxy variables

Identify columns correlated >0.5 with sensitive attributes (e.g., zip code, occupation) and drop or transform them.

Model

Reweight training samples

Apply class_weight='balanced' or fairness-aware reweighting to penalize biased predictions during training.

Model

Per-group threshold calibration

Tune decision thresholds per subgroup post-training to equalize true-positive rates without retraining.
